Latte Coffee

The Perfect Blend of Espresso and Milk

The latte is a beloved coffee drink that combines rich espresso with steamed milk, topped with a layer of microfoam. It’s creamy, smooth, and perfect for those who enjoy a lighter coffee experience. The latte has become a staple in coffee culture around the world, often enjoyed at cafes or made at home with your favorite espresso machine.

Espresso

The foundation of the latte, providing a strong, rich coffee flavor.

Steamed Milk

The milk is heated and steamed to create a creamy, smooth texture.

Microfoam

A layer of frothy milk that sits on top, creating a silky finish.

How to Make a Latte

  1. Prepare a shot of espresso using your espresso machine or stovetop espresso maker.
  2. Steam the milk using a steamer wand, creating a creamy texture and a small amount of froth.
  3. Pour the steamed milk into the espresso, holding back the foam with a spoon to pour the milk first.
  4. Top with a thin layer of microfoam, swirling it gently to create a nice finish.
  5. Optionally, add a sprinkle of cocoa powder, cinnamon, or even caramel for a flavored twist!

Enjoy Your Smooth and Creamy Latte! ☕
