Cappuccino

A Classic Italian Coffee

A Cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It is traditionally served in a small cup and enjoyed throughout the day. The perfect balance between the bitterness of the espresso and the creaminess of the milk foam makes it a favorite among coffee lovers.

Espresso

Strong, rich coffee made from finely ground beans.

Steamed Milk

Heated milk with a velvety texture for smoothness.

Milk Foam

Light and fluffy foam that sits on top for that signature look.
