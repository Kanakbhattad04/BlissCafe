Espresso

The Essence of Coffee

Espresso is a strong, concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans under high pressure. It forms the base for many popular coffee drinks like cappuccinos, lattes, and macchiatos. With its bold flavor and rich crema, espresso is beloved by coffee enthusiasts worldwide.

Step 1: Grind Coffee

Grind high-quality coffee beans into a fine consistency, ideal for espresso.

Step 2: Tamp Coffee

Press the ground coffee evenly into the espresso machine's portafilter using a tamper.

Step 3: Brew Espresso

Place the portafilter in the machine and brew under high pressure for a rich and concentrated shot of espresso.
