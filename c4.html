Frappe Coffee

The Ultimate Iced Coffee Experience

Frappe coffee is the perfect chilled coffee beverage, made from instant coffee, ice, sugar, and water or milk, blended to create a frothy and refreshing drink. Originating in Greece, the frappe has become a worldwide favorite for coffee lovers, especially in the summer months. It's a cool, energizing treat that combines rich coffee flavor with smooth, icy texture.

Instant Coffee

The base of the drink, offering strong coffee flavor and richness.

Ice

Ice cubes to create that refreshing, chilled experience.

Sugar

Sweeten the drink to your liking, making the perfect balance of flavors.
