Croissant

A Flaky Delight from France

The croissant is a buttery, flaky, and delicious pastry that originated in Austria but became synonymous with French baking. Known for its distinctive crescent shape, it is enjoyed globally for breakfast or as a snack, often paired with coffee or jam. The delicate layers of dough create a satisfying crunch and tender crumb in each bite.

Flour

The base ingredient for the dough, creating a soft, elastic texture.

Butter

Generously layered to create the signature flaky texture and rich flavor.

Yeast

Used to leaven the dough and create a light and airy crumb.
